How Can i Setup My IPTV Subscription in SMART STB Device?

Smart STB is an app that allows users to turn their Smart TV into a set-top box for IPTV services. It is available for download on most Smart TV app stores and offers a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate. With Smart STB, users can access a wide range of IPTV channels and services, including live TV, VOD, and catch-up TV. The app also offers advanced features such as time-shifting, recording, and an electronic program guide (EPG) that makes it easy to find and schedule your favorite programs. Overall, Smart STB is a great option for anyone looking to cut the cord and stream their favorite TV shows and movies.

Setting up your OpticTV IPTV subscription on your Smart STB device is a quick and easy process. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Purchase an OpticTV subscription

The first step is to purchase an OpticTV subscription. You can do this by visiting the OpticTV website and selecting the subscription package that best suits your needs. Once you have made the payment, you will receive an email containing the login credentials for your account.

Step 2: Install the Smart STB app

The next step is to install the Smart STB app on your device. This app is available on the app store of your Smart TV, and you can download it for free. Once the app is installed, open it and follow the on-screen instructions to configure the app.

Step 3: Set up your virtual MAC address

The Smart STB app requires a virtual MAC address to function properly. You can obtain your virtual MAC address by following the steps below:

  • 1-Open the Smart STB app on your device.
  • 2-Go to the settings menu.
  • 3-Select ‘Device Info’.
  • 4-Write down the virtual MAC address displayed on the screen.

Step 4: Configure the Smart STB app

Once you have obtained your virtual MAC address, you need to configure the Smart STB app to access your OpticTV subscription. Follow the steps below to do this:

  • 1-Open the Smart STB app on your device.
  • 2-Go to the settings menu.
  • 3-Select ‘System Settings’.
  • 4-Select ‘Portal Settings’.
  • 5-Enter the portal URL for OpticTV that you received via email after purchasing the subscription.
  • 6-Enter your OpticTV login credentials in the ‘Login’ and ‘Password’ fields.
  • 7-Enter the virtual MAC address that you obtained in Step 3.
  • 8-Click on ‘OK’ to save the settings.

Step 5: Access your OpticTV subscription

Once you have configured the Smart STB app, you can access your OpticTV subscription by following the steps below:

  • 1-Open the Smart STB app on your device.
  • 2-Wait for the app to load.
  • 3-Select the OpticTV portal that you configured in Step 4.
  • 4-Wait for the portal to load. This may take a few minutes.
  • 5-Once the portal is loaded, you can access all the channels and shows available on the OpticTV platform.
  • 6- Congratulations! Your OpticTv IPTV subscription is now availiable on your SMART STB app.

Setting up your OpticTV IPTV subscription on your Smart STB device is a simple process that can be completed in just a few minutes. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can access all the benefits that come with the service, including access to a wide range of TV channels and shows. So, grab your remote and start streaming your favorite content today!
