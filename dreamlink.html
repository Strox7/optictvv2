How Can i Setup My IPTV Subscription in My DREAM LINK Device?

Dreamlink is a brand of set-top boxes designed for streaming IPTV content. These devices are designed to work with IPTV services, offering a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily access and stream live TV channels and on-demand content. Dreamlink set-top boxes are known for their reliability and advanced features, such as time-shifting, recording, and an electronic program guide (EPG). They are also known for their compatibility with a wide range of IPTV services, making them a popular choice for IPTV subscribers. Overall, Dreamlink devices provide an easy and efficient way to stream IPTV content on your TV, without the need for a separate computer or other streaming device.

Setting up your OpticTV IPTV subscription on your Dreamlink device is a simple process that can be completed in just a few steps. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Install the DreamOnline app

The first step is to install the DreamOnline app on your Dreamlink device. DreamOnline is the official IPTV app for Dreamlink devices and is available for download on the app store. Once downloaded, install the app and restart your device.

Step 2: Configure the DreamOnline app

Once the DreamOnline app is installed, you need to configure it to access your OpticTV subscription. Follow these steps to configure the app:

  • 1-Open the DreamOnline app on your Dreamlink device.
  • 2-Go to the settings menu.
  • 3-Select ‘Add Portal’.
  • 4-Enter the URL for the OpticTV portal that you received via email or message after purchasing the subscription.
  • 5-Enter your OpticTV login credentials in the ‘Username’ and ‘Password’ fields.
  • 6-Click on ‘Save’ to save the settings.

Step 3: Access your OpticTV subscription

  • 1-Once the DreamOnline app is configured, you can access your OpticTV subscription by following these steps:
  • 2-Open the DreamOnline app on your Dreamlink device.
  • 3-Wait for the app to load.
  • 4-Select the OpticTV portal that you configured in Step 2.
  • 5-Wait for the portal to load. This may take a few minutes.
  • 6-Once the portal is loaded, you can access all the channels and shows available on the OpticTV platform.

Setting up your OpticTV IPTV subscription on your Dreamlink device is a quick and easy process that can be completed in just a few steps. By following the instructions outlined in this article, you can start streaming your favorite TV channels and shows on your Dreamlink device.
